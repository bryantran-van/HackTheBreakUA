Delivering supplies to those in need

Millions of people are experiencing a catastrophic humanitarian catastrophe as the military assault in Ukraine intensifies, and they need immediate aid. We can support communities afflicted by this horrific violence, as well as areas willing to assist displaced individuals, if we work together. Lionheart connects courageous volunteers with people who need help in Ukraine.
